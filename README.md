# Brotato: Fire Sale

A mod for the game [Brotato](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1942280/Brotato/) that occasionally turns the between-wave shop into a timed **Fire Sale** — everything 90% off, free rerolls, and a countdown before prices snap back up.

## How It Works

Each time you enter the shop there's a small chance (8% by default, from wave 2 on, and never two shops in a row) that it becomes a Fire Sale. When it does:

- **Everything is 90% off** — every item and weapon in the shop.
- **3 free rerolls** to dig for what you want, shown right on the reroll button as `0 (3)`.
- A **20-second countdown** — when it hits zero, prices snap back to normal and any unused free rerolls expire.

The shop title becomes a pulsing **FIRE SALE** banner with the live countdown, flashing red and ticking down in the final seconds. Grab the deals before time runs out!

Your selling/recycling value is never discounted, and your own earned free rerolls (for example from Dangerous Bunny) are never touched or spent by the sale — the sale's free rerolls come from a separate pool and are used first.

## Features

- **Random flash-sale event** with a configurable trigger chance and a no-back-to-back cooldown
- **90% off all items and weapons**, stacking correctly with existing discount items
- **Free rerolls** from a dedicated pool, spent before your own so your earned rerolls stay safe
- **Live countdown** with a pulsing red banner and tick sounds in the final seconds
- **Full event presentation** — a trigger sting, a slam-in intro, a screen flash, a pulsing background tint, and coin particles (each toggleable, and the shake respects your Screen Shake setting)
- **Survives save/load** — quit mid-sale and continue your run; the sale resumes with the correct time left
- **Co-op support** — one shared countdown with per-player rerolls and discounts
- **Configurable** — trigger chance, duration, discount, free rerolls, minimum wave, plus on/off toggles for sound, particles, and the intro flourish
